Featured Hotels

The Taj Hotel

The Taj Hotel (Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai) is a luxury heritage hotel overlooking the Arabian Sea, known for its iconic Indo-Saracenic architecture. It symbolizes timeless elegance, world-class hospitality, and India’s rich cultural legacy.

The ITC Hotels

ITC Hotels is a premium Indian hotel chain known for luxury, sustainability, and distinctive regional architecture. It offers world-class hospitality while promoting eco-friendly practices and authentic Indian experiences.

Mayfair Hotels & Resorts

Mayfair Hotels & Resorts is a luxury hospitality brand known for elegant design, personalized service, and serene locations across India. It offers a blend of modern comfort and rich cultural aesthetics for a premium stay experience.

Featured Activities

Himalayan Trekking

Explore the majestic Himalayas with guided trekking routes through Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Sikkim, offering breathtaking views and adventure experiences.

Backwater Houseboat Ride

Enjoy a peaceful houseboat cruise through the scenic backwaters of Kerala, surrounded by lush greenery, calm waters, and traditional village life.

Desert Safari

Experience the thrill of a desert safari in Rajasthan with camel rides, sand dune adventures, cultural performances, and stunning sunset views.

Beach & Water Sports

Relax or enjoy exciting water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, and snorkeling along the beautiful beaches of Goa, Tamil Nadu, and Andhra Pradesh.

Wildlife Safari

Discover India’s rich wildlife through jungle safaris in national parks of Madhya Pradesh and Assam, home to tigers, elephants, and rare species.

Cultural & Heritage Tours

Explore India’s cultural heritage by visiting historical monuments, temples, and festivals across Uttar Pradesh, Odisha, and West Bengal.
